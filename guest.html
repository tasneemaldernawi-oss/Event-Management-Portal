Welcome to LIMU University

The Libyan International Medical University (LIMU) is one of the leading private universities in Libya, known for its modern approach to education and strong academic foundation.

About the University

Established in 2007, LIMU has grown to become a center of excellence in higher education, providing a wide range of programs in medicine, pharmacy, dentistry, and information technology. contact us for more information.

Faculties and Specializations

The university offers a variety of disciplines, including Medicine, Dentistry, Pharmacy, Information Technology, and Business Administration — each designed to prepare students for both local and international success.

Our Teaching Methods

LIMU adopts a modern learning approach that emphasizes problem-based learning (PBL), student participation, and the use of digital technologies to enhance the educational experience.

Community & Volunteer Work

Beyond academics, LIMU takes great pride in its community engagement and volunteer programs. The university frequently organizes activities such as blood donation campaigns, Ramadan aid drives, and autism awareness seminars. Through these initiatives, LIMU seeks to instill in its students a sense of compassion, social responsibility, and active citizenship — values that extend beyond the classroom and into the heart of society.

Events & Activities

LIMU regularly organizes various academic and social events throughout the year, including scientific conferences, workshops, student competitions, and cultural days. These events aim to strengthen the students, academic knowledge while enriching their social and cultural experiences.
